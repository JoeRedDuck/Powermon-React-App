Privacy Policy

Powermon — Prozomix Limited

Effective date: 27 February 2026

Prozomix Limited (“we”, “us”, or “the Company”) operates the Powermon mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have in relation to that information.

1. About Powermon

Powermon is an internal business utility used by employees of Prozomix Limited to monitor power consumption and operational status of factory equipment. The App is distributed as an unlisted application on the Apple App Store and is intended solely for authorised employees.

Access to the App’s backend services requires a connection to the company’s internal network via a secure VPN and valid employee credentials. The backend API is not accessible from the public internet.

2. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When your account is created you provide a username, email address, and password. Your password is stored only as a cryptographic hash and cannot be read by anyone, including system administrators.

b) Device & Equipment Data

The App collects power-consumption readings and operational status from factory monitoring hardware (e.g. freeze-dryers, industrial freezers). This data relates to company equipment, not to your personal devices.

c) Push-Notification Tokens

If you enable push notifications, the App stores an Expo push-notification token so we can deliver critical alerts (power outages, anomalous readings, monitoring-device failures). No other information about your personal device is collected via this token.

d) Notification Preferences

The App stores your mute/unmute preferences for specific machines so that alerts are delivered according to your choices.

e) Information We Do Not Collect

  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contacts, photos, or media from your personal device
  • Browsing history or advertising identifiers
  • Health, fitness, or biometric data
  • Analytics or usage-tracking data sent to third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and grant access to the App.
  • Alerting: To send time-critical push notifications about equipment status.
  • Equipment Monitoring: To display real-time and historical power data for factory machines.
  • Preference Management: To respect your notification mute settings.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the operation of Powermon.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party. The only external service involved is Expo Push Notifications (operated by Expo / 650 Industries), which receives your push-notification token solely to deliver alerts on our behalf.

All other data remains on infrastructure controlled by Prozomix Limited and is accessible only via the company’s secured internal network.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All communication between the App and the backend API occurs over the company’s private network, protected by VPN.
  • Passwords are hashed using Argon2, a modern and secure hashing algorithm.
  • Access tokens are short-lived; refresh tokens can be revoked at any time.
  • The backend database is not exposed to the public internet.

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as you are an active employee and require access to the App. You may delete your account at any time from within the App, which permanently removes your user record and all associated authentication tokens.

Equipment monitoring data (power readings, machine status) is retained for operational and historical analysis purposes and is not linked to individual user accounts.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Correction: You can view your account details within the App and request corrections by contacting us.
  • Notification Control: You can mute notifications for specific machines or disable push notifications entirely via your device settings.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account directly from the App at any time.
  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of your personal data by contacting us at the address below.

8. BYOD (Bring Your Own Device)

Powermon operates on a Bring Your Own Device model. The App does not access, modify, or collect any data from your personal device beyond what is described in this policy. Installing or removing the App is entirely at your discretion.

9. Children’s Privacy

Powermon is a workplace tool and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 16.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
